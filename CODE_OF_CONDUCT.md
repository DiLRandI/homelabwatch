# Code of Conduct

HomelabWatch is an engineering-focused open-source project. The standard is
straightforward: be respectful, be clear, and help move the work forward.

## Expected Behavior

- discuss ideas and code directly without personal attacks
- assume good intent, but be precise when something is risky or incorrect
- give actionable feedback instead of vague dismissal
- keep collaboration welcoming to first-time contributors and operators

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal insults or hostile pile-ons
- publishing private information without consent
- intentionally derailing discussions or support threads

## Moderation

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or close comments, issues, or pull
requests that violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a
temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.
